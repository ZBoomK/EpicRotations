
These are the commands that can be used with the Augmentation Profile **Replace xxxxx with the first 5 letters of Aimsharp Addon Name**.
- To save cooldowns On/Off by deffault off
/xxxxx SaveCooldowns
- To activate out of combat dps by deffault off
/xxxxx OOC
- To queue Renewing Blaze @mouseover on next GCD
/xxxxx RenewingBlaze
- To Rescue @mouseover require 2nd click to fly to
/xxxxx Rescue
- To queue Source of Magic @mousever on next GCD
/xxxxx SourceOfMagic
- To queue Landslide @cursor on next GCD
/xxxxx Landslide
- To queue Expunge @mouseover on next GCD
/xxxxx Expunge
- To queue Cauterizing @mouseover on next GCD
/xxxxx Cauterizing
- To queue Time Skip
/xxxxx TimeSkip
- To queue Tail Swipe
/xxxxx TailSwipe
- To queue Wing Buffet
/xxxxx WingBuffet
- To queue Quell
/xxxxx Quell
- To queue Opressing Roar
/xxxxx OpressingRoar
- To queue Breath of Eons @cursor
/xxxxx BreathOfEons
- To Hold Automatic usage Breath of Eons if selected in configure
/xxxxx HoldEons
- To Hold auto interupts
/xxxxx HoldInterrupt
- To hold auto dispel
/xxxxx HoldDispel
